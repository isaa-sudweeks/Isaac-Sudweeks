School Project

Mini Mars Rover Robot

Team-built rover that completed autonomous tasks using sensor input and PID control.

Robotics Controls Embedded

Overview

The rover navigated a course with stepper motors, IR sensing, and onboard control. I focused on embedded integration and debugging to keep the robot stable under changing loads.

What I built

  • PIC24 firmware for sensor fusion and PID tuning.
  • Power architecture with buck converters and op-amps.
  • Field testing routines for competition tasks.

What I learned

  • How small hardware changes affect control stability.
  • Team integration workflows for robotics builds.
  • Testing autonomy under real-world noise.
