Work Project

Webbing Counter

Designed and built a production-ready counter that dispenses precise lengths of webbing fabric for manufacturing workflows.

Automation Embedded Manufacturing

Overview

The webbing counter automates a manual cutting step by measuring fabric length with rotary encoders, driving an AC motor, and stopping precisely at the programmed distance. The rig reduced operator time and improved repeatability on the production line.

What I built

  • Control software on a Raspberry Pi with encoder feedback.
  • Electrical enclosure with motor drivers and safety controls.
  • Calibration workflow for multiple webbing materials.

What I learned

  • Noise mitigation for encoders in electrically noisy spaces.
  • Designing for maintainability in a busy shop environment.
  • Balancing precision with throughput targets.
