Graduate Research Directions in Robotics

A working list of questions and potential thesis directions in autonomy, perception, and human-centered robotics.

Robotics Research Ideas Grad School

Summary

This idea piece documents research directions I am tracking as I prepare for graduate applications. It focuses on problems where autonomy and real-world deployment intersect.

Key ideas

  • Resilient navigation in low-visibility environments.
  • Human-in-the-loop data collection for robot learning.
  • Efficient embedded perception for small platforms.

Excerpt

The most compelling robotics problems sit at the boundary between what a robot can sense today and what it must infer tomorrow.

What I learned

  • Turning vague interests into researchable questions.
  • Balancing ambition with feasible experiment design.
  • How to communicate a research narrative.
