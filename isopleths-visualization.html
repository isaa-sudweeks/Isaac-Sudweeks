Overview

The project focused on converting large experimental datasets into isopleth surfaces that reveal pollutant behavior. I built the workflow for modeling, visualization, and reporting.

What I built

  • Statistical modeling scripts for high-volume datasets.
  • 3D visualization outputs for research posters.
  • Reusable reporting pipeline for future experiments.

What I learned

  • Framing data stories for scientific audiences.
  • Managing uncertainty in experimental datasets.
  • Balancing model fidelity with visual clarity.
